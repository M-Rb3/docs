## Governance Structure

<Card title="Token-Weighted Governance" icon="coins" horizontal>
  Governance power is proportional to $CENT token holdings and staking amounts,
  ensuring fair representation.
</Card>

### Governance Mechanisms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proposal Creation">
    Any token holder can create governance proposals for platform improvements.
    <Check>Democratic participation in platform development.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Discussion Period">
    Community discusses proposals with feedback and suggestions.
    <Check>Transparent decision-making process with community input.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Voting Period">
    Token holders vote on proposals with power proportional to holdings.
    <Check>Fair representation based on token ownership and commitment.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Implementation">
    Approved proposals are implemented by the development team.
    <Check>Community-driven platform development and feature additions.</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
