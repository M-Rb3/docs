## Economic Model

<Card title="Sustainable Growth" icon="chart-line" horizontal>
  Tokenomics designed for long-term sustainability with deflationary mechanisms
  and value creation.
</Card>

### Value Creation Mechanisms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fee Revenue">
    Platform fees from forex, payments, and exchange services generate revenue.
    <Check>Sustainable revenue model based on ecosystem usage.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Token Buybacks">
    A percentage of revenue is used to buy back $CENT tokens from the market.
    <Check>Regular buybacks create upward price pressure.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Token Burns">
    Purchased tokens are permanently burned, reducing total supply.
    <Check>Deflationary mechanism increases scarcity and value.</Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Value Appreciation">
    Reduced supply and increased demand drive token value appreciation.
    <Check>Long-term value creation for all token holders.</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deflationary Mechanisms

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Buyback Program" icon="coins">

  <Accordion title="Burn Events" icon="fire">
