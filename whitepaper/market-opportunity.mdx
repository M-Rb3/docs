## Current Market Problems

- **Multiple Platforms:** Users need separate accounts for forex, payments, and crypto trading
- **Complex Onboarding:** Each platform requires separate KYC, verification, and setup processes  
- **Inconsistent Fees:** Different fee structures and payment methods across platforms
- **Limited Integration:** No unified dashboard or cross-platform benefits

## Cent's Solution

Cent solves this with a **unified token layer** for finance.

### Unified Platform Benefits

- **Single Account Access:** One account provides access to forex trading, crypto payments, and exchange services
- **Token-Powered Incentives:** $CENT token provides fee discounts, staking rewards, and governance rights across all services  
- **Cross-Platform Integration:** Seamless movement of funds and data between forex, payments, and exchange services
