## Platform Overview

The **Cent Token Platform** is the next evolution of our ecosystem, introducing **retail dashboards, corporate tools, and a governance-ready admin panel** powered by $CENT. This creates a unified token-powered ecosystem where individuals and businesses transact, trade, and grow together.
